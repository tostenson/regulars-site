The Story

You know every regular by name.

The diner opens at sundown and runs until something or someone closes it. You seat the booths, take the tickets, work the grill, and ring the register. It is the same nightly routine with the same familiar faces.

But not every customer who walked in is who they say they are. Your notebook tracks what you know about each regular, and comparing it against who's sitting in the booths is how you find the irregular before the shift ends.
